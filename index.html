What is Servant Leadership?

Servant leadership is a philosophy where the leader serves first, empowering others to reach their full potential. At IMSA, this practice enables students to develop empathy, foster collaboration, and drive meaningful change.

Applications of Servant Leadership

Student Leadership Council

Advocating for students, organizing events, and shaping IMSA’s culture.

Peer Tutoring

Fostering academic excellence and mutual growth through collaborative learning.

Community Outreach

Building bridges and creating impactful change through service projects.

Residential Life

Encouraging inclusivity and shared responsibilities in student living spaces.
